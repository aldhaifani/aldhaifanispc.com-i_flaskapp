Conditions for Valid Claims


1. Strict Adherence to Application Instructions:

The claimant must have diligently followed the application instructions provided with the Super Gel product. This includes adhering to the recommended dosage, application techniques, and targeted areas specified in the instructions. Any deviation from these guidelines may impact the effectiveness of the product and invalidate the claim.

2. Visual and Written Evidence

Substantiate any claim with both visual and written evidence demonstrating the alleged ineffectiveness.

Visual Evidence: Provide clear, high-resolution images or videos of treated areas both before and after application. These visuals should clearly depict the presence of cockroaches or ants, as well as the application of Super Gel. Time-stamped images or videos are preferred to accurately document the progression.

Written Evidence: Alongside visual evidence, furnish a detailed written account of the application process. Include dates, times, and specific steps followed during the application. This written evidence serves to provide context and further corroborate the effectiveness of the product.


Submission Process for Claims:

1. Contact Us: Promptly reach out to our customer support team within a reasonable timeframe from the initial application. Communication channels include email, phone, or our online contact form

2. Provide Comprehensive Evidence: Compile and submit comprehensive visual and written evidence to support the claim. Ensure that all evidence is clear, accurate, and relevant to the issue at hand.

3. Thorough Review Process: Our dedicated team will conduct a thorough review of the submitted evidence. This review process involves meticulous scrutiny to assess the validity of the claim and determine the appropriate course of action.

4. Resolution: If the claim is deemed valid based on the evidence provided, appropriate measures will be taken to address the issue. Potential resolutions may include a product replacement, refund, or alternative remedy as deemed suitable by Aldhaifani Trading and Investment Co.



Note: Terms and conditions of Aldhaifani Trading and Investment Co apply to all claims submitted under this policy.
